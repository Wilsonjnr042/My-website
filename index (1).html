Support & FAQ

How long does it take to build a website?
The timeline for building a website depends on its complexity and features. A basic website typically takes 2-4 weeks, while more complex projects with custom functionality may take 6-8 weeks or longer. After our initial consultation, I'll provide a detailed project timeline.
Do you offer website maintenance?
Yes, I offer comprehensive maintenance packages that include regular updates, security monitoring, backups, and technical support. Maintenance plans are tailored to your specific needs and can be discussed after your website launch.
Can you help with website content?
While my primary focus is design and development, I can assist with content strategy and provide guidance on creating effective website copy. For professional copywriting services, I work with trusted partners and can facilitate that collaboration.
What is your payment process?
I typically require a 30% deposit to begin work, with the balance due upon project completion. For larger projects, we can arrange milestone payments. All payment details will be clearly outlined in our contract.
Do you work with international clients?
Absolutely! I work with clients worldwide. Communication is primarily conducted via email, video calls, and project management tools to ensure smooth collaboration regardless of location.
